Ecosys_SouthernBluefinTunaR Documentation

Southern Bluefin Tuna in Australian Waters

Description

National data for Australia's ecosystem services: fisheries biomass provisioning services (2000-01 to 2020-21). v9. CSIRO. Data Collection.

Format

csv file with 22 rows and 4 columns

Details

  • Fin Year: time variable for financial year 1 July to 30 June

  • Catch: kilograms caught per year

  • GVP: Gross value product in dollars as market price times catch

  • EV: Exchange value in dollars as the value of ecosystem services as if a market existed

Regions are aggregated for all of Australia.

Author(s)

Pascoe, Sean; Liu, Ning; Scheufele, Gabriela; Tetreault Campbell, Sally; & Schmidt, Becky, 2024

Source

https://doi.org/10.25919/nzrp-6702
