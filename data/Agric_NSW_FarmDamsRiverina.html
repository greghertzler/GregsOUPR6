Agric_NSW_FarmDamsRiverinaR Documentation

Water in Farm Dams in the Riverina of New South Wales

Description

Stock water management to improve drought resilience in intensive-use grazing landscapes.

Format

csv file with 19313 rows and 4 columns

Details

  • Run Days: time variable in daily increments

  • Baseline: water volumes in cubic metres without a windbreak

  • TwentyPct scenario: water volumes in cubic metres with a windbreak to suppress evaporation by 20%

  • FortyPct scenario: water volumes in cubic metres with a windbreak to suppress evaporation by 40%

MatLab simulated water volumes for a 4,000 cubic metre dam.

Author(s)

Helena Clayton, Sally Thompson, Tim Capon, Greg Hertzler, Philip Graham, and David Lindenmayer, 2026
