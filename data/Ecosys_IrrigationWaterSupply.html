Ecosys_IrrigationWaterSupplyR Documentation

Water Supply for Irrigated Agriculture in Eastern Australia

Description

National data for Australia's ecosystem services: irrigated agricultural water supply (250 m resolution): 2000-01 to 2022-23. v7. CSIRO. Data Collection.

Format

csv file with 13 rows and 2 columns

Details

  • Fin Year: time variable for financial year 1 July to 30 June

  • WaterSupply: irrigation water supplied in Australia in megalitres

Regions are aggregated for Eastern Australia.

Author(s)

Liu, Ning; Smith, Greg; Evans, David; Tetreault Campbell, Sally; Pascoe, Sean; & Schmidt, Becky, 2024

Source

https://doi.org/10.25919/7jj7-8826
