Ecosys_SydneyWaterR Documentation

Sydney Drinking Water Catchment

Description

WaterNSW WaterInsight, Water Storage from August 2015 to July 2025

Format

csv file with 120 rows and 14 columns

Details

  • Day: time variable for days from August 2015

  • All: All Storage volume in gigalitres

  • Blue Mtns: Blue Mountains Dams volume in gigalitres

  • Nepean: Nepean Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Avon: Avon Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Wingecarribe: Wingecarribe Reservoir volume in gigalitres

  • Cordeaux: Cordeaux Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Cataract: Cataract Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Warragamba: Warragamba Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Woronora: Woronora Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Prospect: Prospect Reservoir volume in gigalitres

  • Tallowa: Tallowa Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Fitzroy: Fitzroy Falls Dam volume in gigalitres

  • Year: time variable as decimal year

Source

https://waterinsights.waternsw.com.au/12964-sydney-drinking-water-catchment/storage
