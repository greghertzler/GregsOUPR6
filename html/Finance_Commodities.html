Finance_CommoditiesR Documentation

Metals and Energy Commodities

Description

Closing prices on the first day of each month from 1 January 2009 to 1 May 2025.

Format

csv file with 197 rows and 8 columns

Details

  • Day: time variable in days since 1 January 2009

  • Gold: London Bullion Market fix at the Perth (Aus) Mint in USD/troy ounce

  • Silver: London Bullion Market fix in USD/troy ounce

  • Copper: London Metals Exchange spot price, Copper, Grade A, USD/tonne

  • Iron Ore: Cleared for Export Tianjin port, Iron Ore Fines 62% FE spot price in USD/tonne

  • WTI: West Texas Intermediate in USD/bbl

  • Brent: Brent Crude in USD/bbl

  • Year: time variable as decimal year

Source

https://files.marketindex.com.au/files/workbooks/commodities-workbook.xlsx
